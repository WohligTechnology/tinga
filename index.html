The TING Story

Sydney: A sunny autumn day, girl problems, retrospection on life and the urge to start something awesome mothered TING Australia! With existing offices in Mumbai and Chennai in India, TING expanded into the beautiful shores of Australia with an office in sunny Sydney. With a team of energetic and experienced creative professionals TING Australia started off with a modest yet ambitious goal to bring exceptional creative and digital solutions to the vast and growing Australian market. Working on anything that involved design, from web sites, e-commerce portals, apps, displays and cool new techno stuff like augmented reality, all seem to have found a space in TING! TING Australia envisions to be a force to be reckoned with in the creative and digital solutions space and will stop at nothing to offer mind-blowing solutions to Aussie businesses! No fluff, just the good stuff!
